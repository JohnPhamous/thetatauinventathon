Inventathon

The first annual Theta Tau Inventathon will be an informal 8 week hackathon. Participants of all majors are welcomed to participate with prizes being awarded to the best projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Inventathon?

    The inventathon will last 8 weeks starting week 1 of winter 2017. Unlike a traditional hackathon, participants will be able to work on their projects whenever they can during the 8 weeks. Judging will commence at the end.

  • Code? Electronics? Metal?

    We encourage everyone of any major to participate with any projects. Projects from planes to websites will be accepted.

  • Teams?

    Teams can be at most 6 people to qualify for prizes. You can work alone, but it'll be less fun! Teamwork is a valuable skill.
